Karma Community

A Full Stack MEAN app created with Stevan Popovich

Karma Community was our third project during our time on General Assembly's Web Development Immersive Course (week 7-8). It was a group project built by myself and Steve.

The idea was to build a community good-will app that:

  • Allows users to ask favours from members their community.
  • Choose a selected volunteer for a task (with SMS notifications of choice and approval).
  • Gain 'karma coins' based on completed favours.

At the start, trying to make sure that I wasn't hard coding a lot of the early functions so that they could be called on later on in the build process without having to change too much. It took a while to work out the logic to achieve this and I maybe invested a bit too much time in to it and wasn't able to get to some of the more advanced features that I had originally planned that would help players advance further in to the game once the speeds get a lot faster.
